About me
As a psychologist, HR professional and a life coach, I've had the previlege of guiding over 2,000 individuals to become more confident, and empowered in their personal and professional lives.

My approach is centered on creating a safe, open space for my students to express themselves, discover their strengths and unlock their dormant potential. To me, learning is not just about gaining skills. It's also about shaping a more refined, confident and compassionate version of oneself.

At The Crafted One, I'm dedicated to helping you grow holistically by crafting your journey into one of lasting success and fulfillment.
With an MBA from Mahatma Gandhi University and an MS in psychology from the Higher School of Economics, Moscow, I have a strong academic background in understanding different types of people and businesses.

My professional experience is diverse. Initially I started as a sales executive and went on to work as an HR officer at Bharat Heavy Electricals for six years until I secured a fully funded scholarship to do my MS in Russia. In Russia, I worked as a part time English trainer and also obtained a Cambridge certification (CELTA) in teaching English as a foreign language.

After returning to India in 2021, I continued teaching English online and began my journey with Amrita University as a soft skills trainer. I find teaching and mentoring a very fulfilling experience and I am constantly trying to learn new things which I can share with my students.
